worldworstwebsite

worldworstwebsite
  • Contrast: The coloring here has no distinct theme. The color scheme is bright and assults the eyes so much it leaves the viewer disoriented.
  • Repitition: There is little to no repitition here, the fonts are all different, the colors are constantly changing, and the links are largely inconsistant.
  • Alignment: There are literally no visual connections to where I am supposed to go. There are only links scattered around the page in a disoragnized manner.
  • Proximity: The navigation is incredibly disoragnized. Some links lead nowhere, others are found at the bottom of the page. And there is a search bar, but you need to access an unkown area in the website.

Canvas

Canvas
  • Contrast: Here the coloring is relatively uniform. It takes advantage of shading so that it may by aesthetically pleasing for the viewer whilst also staying readible.
  • Repitition: The majority of fonts stay uniform with little changes. All of the visuals are created in such a way where they complement the text rather than conflict with it.
  • Alignment: The visual connections make sense and allow the viewer to more easilly navigate the page.
  • Proximity: Everything is close to where it needs to be and is easilly accessable by the viewer.
